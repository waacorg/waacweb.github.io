World Academy for Artificial Consciousness (WAAC)

The World Academy for Artificial Consciousness (WAAC) is a global, non-governmental academic institution formally established in Paris in 2025. It is devoted to advancing the frontiers of artificial consciousness research by integrating science, technology, and philosophy through worldwide collaboration.

WAAC brings together scholars, technologists, and policymakers from leading institutions across the UK, US, China, and Singapore. Its mission is to explore the evolution of AI toward self-awareness and subjective experience, while developing robust ethical frameworks and international governance standards.
